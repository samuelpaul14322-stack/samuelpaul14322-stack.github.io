Privacy Policy - NutriLens: AI Food Tracker

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Key Point: NutriLens is 100% offline. We collect no personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

NutriLens does not collect any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device only, including: food scan history, meal logs, nutrition tracking data, health goals, and app settings. This data never leaves your device.

2. Data Usage

Local data is used solely to provide app features: displaying nutrition history, health insights, and on-device AI food recognition.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share data with any third parties. NutriLens makes no network connections. Your data is never sold or disclosed.

4. Camera Permission

Camera access is used solely to capture food images for on-device nutritional analysis. Images are never stored or transmitted externally.

5. Security

Your data is protected by your device's security features. Offline-only operation means no transmission risks.

6. Children

NutriLens is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
